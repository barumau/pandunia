
# Lesson 2: Talking about yourself

## Dialog 1

**mi su nim es Tomas.**
– My name is Thomas.  
**mi have 25 (due ten faif) yer.**
– I am 25 years old.  
**mi es studer e sporter.**
– I am a student and a sportsman.  
**mi stude historia.**
– I study history.  
**mi sporte futebol.**
– I play football.  
**mi have pa, ma, un brat e du sis en mi su familia.**
– I have father, mother, a brother and two sisters in my family.

## Dialog 2

<big>🧓</big>
**ke es tu su nim?**
– What's your name?  
<big>👩</big>
**mi su nim es Maria.**
– My name is Maria.  
<big>🧓</big>
**tu have ke multi yer?**
– How old are you?  
<big>👩</big>
**mi have 30 (tri ten) yer.**
– I am 30 years old.  
<big>🧓</big>
**tu fa ke ka profesie?**
– What do you do by profession?  
<big>👩</big>
**mi es un ticer.**
– I am a teacher.  
<big>🧓</big>
**tu tice ke?**
– What do you teach?  
<big>👩</big>
**mi tice matematike en mide skole.**
– I teach mathematics in the secondary school.  
<big>🧓</big>
**tu fa ke ka hobi?**
– What do you do as a hobby?  
<big>👩</big>
**mi stude linguajes.**
– I study languages.
