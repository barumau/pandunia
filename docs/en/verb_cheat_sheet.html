mi yam. I eat.
mi sta yam. I am eating.
mi ha yam. I have eaten.
mi no yam. I don't eat.
mi no sta yam. I'm not eating.
mi no ha yam. I haven't eaten.
mi le yam. I ate.
mi le sta yam. I was eating.
mi le ha yam. I had eaten.
mi no le yam. I didn't eat.
mi no le sta yam. I was not eating.
mi no le ha yam. I had not eaten.
mi fu yam. I will eat.
mi fu sta yam. I will be eating.
mi fu ha yam. I will have eaten.
mi no fu yam. I won't eat.
mi no fu sta yam. I won't be eating.
mi no fu ha yam. I won't have eaten.
mi le fu yam. I was going to eat.
mi no le fu yam. I wasn't going to eat.

mi vud sta yam. I would be eating.
mi vud ha yam. I would have eaten.
mi no vud yam. I wouldn't eat.
mi no vud sta yam. I wouldn't be eating.
mi no vud ha yam. I wouldn't have eaten.
mi debe yam. I should eat.
mi debe sta yam. I should be eating.
mi debe ha yam. I should have eaten.
mi no debe yam. I shouldn't eat.
mi no debe sta yam. I shouldn't be eating.
mi no debe ha yam. I shouldn't have eaten.
mi pote yam. I can eat.
mi pote sta yam. I can be eating.
mi pote ha yam. I may have eaten.
mi le pote yam. I could eat.
mi le pote ha yam. I could have eaten.
mi le pote sta yam. I could have been eating.
mi fu pote yam. I will be able to eat.
mi no pote yam. I can't eat.
mi no pote sta yam. I can't be eating.
mi no pote ha yam. I may not have eaten.
mi no le pote yam. I couldn't eat.
mi no le pote ha yam. I couldn't have eaten.
mi no le pote sta yam. I couldn't have been eating.
mi no fu pote yam. I won't be able to eat.
